BLUEY


Bluey is an animated TV show showing the life and adventures of an Australian Blue Heeler dog named Bluey. The show is centered on how she and her family deal with the everyday struggles of life; whether big or small. This show teaches its viewers important life lessons and concepts.

Bluey Heeler


Bluey, the main character, is a Blue Heeler pup who loves to play. She has a younger sister named Bingo and her parents are Chilli and Bandit Heeler.

Bingo Heeler


Bingo is a Red Heeler pup who is the younger sister of Bluey. She is a very smart and obedient little pup who relies on her sister a lot, since she is only 4 years old. She and Bluey are similar in the fact that they both love to play.

Chilli Heeler


Chilli is a hardworking Red Heeler who is the mother of Bluey and Bingo. She takes care of everyone as well as the house.

Bandit Heeler


Bandit is a Blue Heeler who is the father of Bluey and Bingo. He works and provides income for their family yet always finds time to play with the kids.
